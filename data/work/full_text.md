*Infusions, Liqueurs, Syrups & Dosing Charts --- Digitized from the Notebook*

Karl\'s Apple Pie Liqueur

  ----------------- ----------------------------------
  **Ingredient**    **Amount**
  Apple cider       1 qt
  Apple juice       1 qt
  Sugar             3 cups
  Cinnamon sticks   8, plus 2 fresh ones added later
  GNS (93%)         1 qt

*Boil cider + juice 5--10 minutes. Add sugar and the 8 cinnamon sticks, let cool completely, then stir in the GNS. Add 2 fresh cinnamon sticks before bottling.*

Part 6 --- Conversions & Production Notes

-   1 gallon = 16 cups.

-   GNS = grain neutral spirit, dosed at 93% ABV throughout this book unless noted otherwise.

-   \~7.5 g fresh ginger ≈ 1 inch piece.

-   A typed reference sheet in the notebook cross-checks the RTD percentages: Mango Collins vodka = 12.64% of total volume, Cherry Limeade cherry vodka = 14.29%, Grape Popsicle cran vodka = 13.89% --- these line up with the ABV figures already calculated in the separate RTD Recipes document.

-   Several bulk/commercial-scale entries (Cherry Vodka, Cranberry Vodka, Amaretto multipliers, Coffee Liqueur case batches) had partly illegible figures in the original --- flagged inline above; re-verify against the physical notebook before scaling production.
